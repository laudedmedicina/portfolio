Who I Am

Curiosity Driven by Purpose

A physician-researcher dedicated to advancing evidence-based medicine through rigorous systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and clinical research.

I am a physician (MBBCh) affiliated with Alexandria Faculty of Medicine, Alexandria, Egypt, with a growing research focus in clinical medicine and evidence-based therapeutics. My work centers on synthesizing the best available evidence through systematic reviews and meta-analyses to inform clinical decision-making.

My research portfolio spans diverse areas of medicine including wound management, neurological disorders, and pharmacological interventions. I am particularly interested in evaluating novel therapeutic agents—such as topical wound-care treatments and neuroprotective strategies—in populations where evidence gaps remain.

Complementing my clinical research, I am a certified Associate Data Scientist from DataCamp, with hands-on expertise in statistical analysis and data visualization using R. This quantitative foundation strengthens my systematic reviews and meta-analyses, enabling more robust evidence synthesis and deeper insight from complex medical datasets.

I am committed to collaborative, multidisciplinary research and regularly partner with international teams to produce high-impact evidence syntheses published in peer-reviewed journals across neurology, pharmacology, and clinical medicine.
